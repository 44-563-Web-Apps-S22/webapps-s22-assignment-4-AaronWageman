Play: Tartuffe

Orgon

See?

Elmire

Yes.

Orgon

Fantasy!

Elmire

But if I found a way To make you see it all in light of day?

Orgon

Fairy tales!

Elmire

What a man! At least reply. I don't ask you to believe me, but I Do wonder what you will say of your good man If I bring you to a place where you can Clearly see and hear these things? What then?

Orgon

In that case I would say . . . nothing again, For it cannot be.
